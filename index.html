Our Story


Welcome to Cordus, where meaningful connections and genuine camaraderie thrive. Founded in 1894, Cordus has been a premier destination for individuals seeking a place to unwind, socialize, and enjoy the finer things in life.


At Cordus, we believe in fostering an environment where members can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in an atmosphere of warmth, relaxation, and friendship.

Our Mission & Values


Our mission at Cordus is to create a welcoming environment where individuals can connect, unwind, and forge lasting friendships. We strive to uphold the following core values:

  • Community: Fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our members.
  • Quality: Offering premium amenities, services, and experiences to our community.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and welcoming individuals from all walks of life.
  • Excellence: Committing to excellence in every aspect of our operations and member experience.

Club Facilities


At Cordus, we offer an array of luxurious facilities and amenities to enhance your social experience:

  • Elegant lounge areas for relaxation and conversation.
  • Well-stocked bars featuring a selection of fine spirits and wines.
  • Premium cigar lounge for aficionados to indulge in their passion.
  • Private event spaces for special occasions and gatherings.

Membership Information


Becoming a member of Cordus offers a host of exclusive benefits:

  • Access to our luxurious club facilities and amenities.
  • Invitations to exclusive member-only events and gatherings.
  • Discounts on select products and services.
  • Priority booking for private event spaces.

To inquire about membership and fees, please contact our membership team.
